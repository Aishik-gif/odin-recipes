Pumpkin Pie

Description

Pumpkin pie is a food item that can be eaten by the player

Decent amount of food but needs a lot of crafting

Ingredients

  • 1 Pumpkin
  • 1 sugar
  • 1 egg

Steps

    Place in a 3x3 or 2x2 crafting grid

  1. Place Pumpkin, egg and sugar in any order
