Cake

Description

Cake is a food and a block that can be eaten by the player

Although it is a pretty bad source of food with low saturation and lack of mobility

Ingredients

  • 3 Buckets of milk
  • 2 Sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3 Wheat

Steps

    Place in a 3x3 crafting grid

  1. Place 3 buckets in the top row
  2. Place sugar on the 2nd row, 1st and 3rd column
  3. Place egg in between two sugars
  4. Wheat goes in the 3rd row
